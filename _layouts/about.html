About Me:

I am a Back-End Engineer, Educator, Musician, and Gamer. I like to solve problems, communicate, create, and compete.

I attended the University of Maryland, where I earned a B.A. in English and an M. Ed. in English Education. After graduating, I taught as a public educator for 2 years, working to better the future by equipping young minds.

I entered The Iron Yard's Ruby on Rails Back-End Engineering course in Durham, NC on September 12th, 2016. For twelve weeks, I struggled, failed, and grew on a daily basis. The lessons I learned were hard-earned, but more than worth their costs. The experience was life-changing, and I am grateful that The Iron Yard taught me so much.

For our capstone project, my team created CodeKarma, a platform that promotes technological altruism. Our communication, patience, and persistence enabled us to overcome the many obstacles in our project. Completing CodeKarma taught me the efficiency, potency, and and joy of working with a dynamic, passionate team.

Currently, I am searching for new opportunities to continue honing and applying my software development skills. If you have any information you would like to share, please contact me using one of the provided links! For more about me, feel free to review my resume, Github, and LinkedIn.
